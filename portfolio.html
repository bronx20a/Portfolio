Who I am

My name is Tony, I am a recent college graduate from New England. I learned web design during the pandemic and I have honed my skills to bring my ideas to life. I am the founder of The Central Post, which is a news analysis blog that I designed and published. I graduated from undergraduate study in May 2020, Magna Cum Laude, with a degree in Business and Politics. During my undergraduate career, I conducted extensive research into economic and political trends both domestic and abroad. I received high honors for my thesis research, which focused on African American political participation in southern states after the repeal of certain provisions of the Voting Rights Act.
