Who I am

My name is Tony, I am a freelance front-end web developer from New England. I learned web design during the pandemic and I have honed my skills to bring my ideas to life. I am the founder of The Central Post, which is a news analysis blog that I designed and published. Since the site's launch, I have published eighteen articles across three genres with new content published weekly.
